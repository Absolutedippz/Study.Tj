Parameters for calculating the total score

SL – Scores of lessons during the semester

TE – Test exam

RSL – The ratio of lessons points to sum of IC points = 0.5

RLIE – The ratio of sum of lessons and IC points to the sum of test scores and other types of exam = 0.4

IC1 – First interim control

OE – Other exam

IC2 – Second interim control

Motivative – Scores from the dean's office, for activities in public works and university life (up to 10 points).

RTE – The ratio of test exam scores to another type of exam scores = 0.0

NIC – The number of intermediate control = 2

Formula for calculating the Total score
((SL * RSL) + ((IC1 + IC2) / NIC) * (1 - RSL)) * RLIE + ((TE * RTE) + (OE * (1 - RTE))) * (1 - RLIE) + Motivative scores = Total

Conditions for accessing exams

- The number of unworked absents is less then 3

- The sum of IC scores more than 50

- Lesson scores more than 50

- Scores of both IC should be more than 0

- Minimum Exam Score for gathering Final mark 0

Conditions for getting the Fx final mark

- Result score >= 45

- Test exam score >= 0

- Writing exam score >= 45

Conditions for getting the final mark (D to A)

- Result score >= 50

- Test exam score >= 0

- Other exam score >= 50

Retaking exam

After paying the amount of credits for the subject, the student have to repeat educational material per semester, and gets the opportunity to retake the exam. The points for the lessons and the ICs will not be considered and will not affect the formation of the final score.
